The lions, with regal manes, prowl through faded manuscripts, weaving through words like ships on the sea.

Under ancient lamps, their shadows stretch, and claws trace lines of ink with reverence, uncovering histories, mysteries, and dreams.

In quiet chambers, they find solace in forgotten tales, capturing the essence of humanity.

Satiated, they rest on beds of parchment and ink, their dreams now intertwined with the words they've claimed, forever guardians of knowledge and lore.
